# Tenda ITR 1T25 — Summary

Tenda reported record quarterly results in 1T25, driven by favorable MCMV policies and strong pricing power.

**Revenue and margins:** Consolidated net revenue reached R$ 865.2 mm (+16.2% a/a), with adjusted gross margin of 33.6% (+6.8 p.p. a/a). Excluding Pode Entrar, adjusted gross margin was 34.0%. The Tenda segment hit an all-time high adjusted gross margin of 36.7%.

**Profitability:** Consolidated net income was R$ 85.5 mm (vs R$ 4.4 mm in 1T24), a record quarter. Adjusted EBITDA reached R$ 152.9 mm (+50.5% a/a), also a record. ROE LTM rose to 19.3% and ROCE LTM to 26.5%. Annualized ROE on the Tenda segment alone exceeded 30%.

**Alea segment:** Revenue of R$ 77.3 mm (+40.1% a/a) but adjusted gross margin of only 6.8%, reflecting challenges in stabilizing construction patches. Net loss of R$ 19.4 mm. Sales are improving month-over-month in 2025 (146 units/month in 4T24 to 315 in April). Management expects margin recovery toward 20-24% guidance over the year.

**Balance sheet and debt:** Gross debt fell to R$ 849.1 mm (-22.9% a/a), with duration of 19.9 months and average cost of 12.89% a.a. Net debt of R$ 267.6 mm. Corporate net debt/PL was -1.8%. Total net debt/(PL+minorities) of 24.2%. All covenants met with wide margin.

**Cash generation:** Total cash burn of R$ 37.5 mm (including securitization variation), but recurring operating cash generation for the Tenda segment of R$ 91.6 mm when excluding non-recurring impacts: (i) CEF rule change on repasse vs registro (R$ 33.0 mm) and (ii) delayed repasses in CE and RS states (R$ 55.0 mm).

**Backlog:** Revenue to be recognized of R$ 2,546.5 mm, results to be recognized of R$ 869.7 mm, with adjusted REF margin of 37.3% (40.7% ex Pode Entrar), signaling continued margin expansion.

**MCMV update (April 2025):** FGTS approved higher income brackets (Faixa 1 to R$ 2,850, creation of Faixa 4 up to R$ 12,000), expanded property price limits, and equalized tiers for smaller municipalities. These changes are structurally positive for Tenda's addressable market.

**Key concern:** Alea's margin trajectory. MBA of 6.8% is far below the 20-24% guidance. Rapid growth (10 to 22 active sites a/a) is creating execution challenges. Management is focused on internalizing finishing processes into the factory to regain efficiency.
