# Conference Call: Chip Distributor Expert Call with Kevin Wang

Kevin Wang is the Managing Director for the Asia-Pacific (APAC) region at Sourceability, a company specializing in digital supply chain solutions for electronic components. He joined Sourceability in 2020, bringing with him extensive experience from his previous role at Future Electronics, where he served as Vice President and initially as Sales Director for the company's FAI division.

Wang's career in the semiconductor industry has provided him with a global perspective on component sourcing and supply chain management, having worked in various regions including Munich, North America, Japan, and Hong Kong. At Sourceability, Wang is responsible for driving the company's initiatives in the APAC region, focusing on the digitalization of the supply chain through online B2B distribution platforms like Sourcengine, which allows customers to compare and purchase from a wide range of vetted vendors globally.

His appointment is part of Sourceability's strategic efforts to enhance support and growth in the APAC region, leveraging his deep knowledge of the semiconductor industry and his proven track record in overseeing consistent sales growth.
