# Nubank Ximena Salgado

Worked at Banxico in financial inclusion.

Worked at Mckinsey as well in financial inclusion projects from private and public sector.

Worked  at Amazon.

First product manager at Nubank in Mexico, 2019.

Criterias to choose from next countries?

We were evaluating only countries in Latin America at that time.

Looking at market size, profitability of the banking sector...

Main barriers to expand credit card portfolio in the country?

Things have changed a lot since the public launch of the credit card. The main challenge initially was the informality and scarcity of realiable data.

Models have evolved to correctly predict the risk of the client,

When nucuenta launched, it didn't pay a lot, and got a lot of customers. When it started to pay 15% that deposits exploded and the people with income came.

I think it is a matter of time for nu to crack the code in the mass market.

40% of users that had a secured credit card got an offer for unsecured in less than two years.

Mercadolibre vs Nu?

Mercado pago has the merchant part, smbs. Mercado pago has more features,

but nubank has a better credit card product.

Profitability looking at the credit card mass market?

One thing that was worrysome was the npls. Non performing loans

were less of a concern from the beggining.

Revolving interest is something that has changed on nubank. It is something that we

were training the models from the beggining. I don't think it is very different from the market.

If you look at nubank's rate, it was lower than the other competitors like azteca that charges more.
