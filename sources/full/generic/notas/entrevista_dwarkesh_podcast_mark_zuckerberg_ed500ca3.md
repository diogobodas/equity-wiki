What we will be using the datacenters for?

The future of products will involve more complex tasks and a greater number of agents, rather than just chatbots. Agent AI will play a significant role in the future, similar to how AI will cater to specific needs of communities in various businesses. There is anticipation that AI could be trained by creators on the platform to generate content, indicating a potential shift in content creation processes.

Our ratio of inference compute to training is probably much higher than other companies just because of the sheer volume of interactions we have in community.

No one knows for how long this exponential growth of GPU investing will keep going.

In the past even companies that had infinite amount of money could not get as much gpus as they wanted, and i think this is starting to ease, but i think before this happens, we will reach energy constraints.

Getting energy is way slower than investing in software and hardware.

We would buy biggers clusters than we are building now if we could source more energy.

I think the generation of synthetic data to feed training will increase steadily.
